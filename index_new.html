About

Built for classrooms where installing software is the hard part.

PanPhy Labs exists because browser-based tools are often the only tools everyone can actually open. On school-managed devices, the friction is rarely understanding physics. It is permissions, missing installs, blocked software, and the overhead of making a room full of machines behave.

These apps are designed to cut through that. They are lightweight, direct, and made to feel useful immediately. Most of them are cached for offline use after the first visit, so the site keeps working even when the network does not.

Offline-first

Fifteen experiences keep working after the first successful cache.

Look for the offline badge on supported cards. Those pages and their core assets are stored locally by the service worker.

Built with AI

The point is not novelty. The point is shipping useful, browser-native tools quickly.

AI accelerates iteration, but the bar here is still practical classroom value and clear interaction design.

Open by default

No accounts for students. No installer. No platform lock-in.

Everything is delivered as a static web app, which keeps deployment simple and access broad.
