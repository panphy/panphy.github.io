About The Project

Why PanPhy Labs Exists

A classroom-focused collection of web apps built to be easy to open, easy to share, and useful in real teaching.

The goal is not to be a giant portal. It is to provide a dependable set of apps that teachers and students can actually use.

Built for school devices

Many teaching environments do not allow software installs or complex setup. PanPhy Labs focuses on browser-based tools that open quickly and behave well on managed laptops, tablets, and phones.

The site mixes practical classroom utilities, concept-first physics simulations, and a few lighter experiments where a playful interface helps learners engage.

Offline where it matters

Most published pages cache after the first visit. When a card shows Offline ready, that page and its core assets are available without a network connection.

A small number of features still depend on live services, especially Asteroid Storm's leaderboard and the external EAL Companion app.
