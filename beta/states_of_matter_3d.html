Phase Guide

What You Are Seeing

Solid Particles stay near fixed lattice sites and mainly vibrate in place.
Liquid Particles remain close together but slide past one another, so the regular pattern disappears.
Gas Particles move rapidly and spread through the whole container volume with only brief collisions.
